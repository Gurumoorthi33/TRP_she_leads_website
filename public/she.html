🏫 About Us

The SheLeads@TRP Initiative

SheLeads@TRP is a flagship women empowerment initiative by SRM TRP Engineering College, Tiruchirappalli. Launched to celebrate the brilliance of women in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, SheLeads@TRP creates a powerful platform for students to discover, compete, and grow.

This curated tracker brings together 107 real opportunities — scholarships, hackathons, and internships — from top organizations worldwide, all handpicked for women who dare to lead.
